Confirmed vs. Possible vs. everything else

Three mutually exclusive groups: Confirmed (the complaint's own text names a bike lane), Possible (no such text, but the location sits within 15m of a mapped bike lane), and Other (the rest of the same Illegal Parking / Abandoned Vehicle / Bicycle Issues / Abandoned Bicycle pool). Methodology & caveats

  • Confirmed is a text match on the complaint description (bike/bicycle/cyclist/cycling + lane/path/pathway, or "bikeway") — not a verified determination.
  • Possible is geo-proximity only (within 15m of Boston's official on-street bike lane network) — a broader, lower-precision signal than Confirmed; some fraction of it is ordinary parking complaints that happen to be near a bike lane street.
  • Street/neighborhood/ward names come from resolving each request's coordinates against Boston's bike lane network and a bulk 311 export — a small number of records may show as "Unknown" or lack a ward if no match was found.
  • Boston is mid-migration to a new 311 backend (started Oct 2025, expected complete by mid-2026); some service types may be inconsistently represented between systems during this period.
